## Overview

Currently leading the design and implementation of a comprehensive ABM program for a mid-market B2B SaaS company looking to move upmarket into enterprise accounts.

## Challenge

The company had successfully grown through inbound and outbound motions targeting SMB and mid-market. Moving into enterprise required a fundamentally different approach:

- Longer sales cycles with multiple stakeholders
- Need for personalized, multi-touch engagement
- Coordination between marketing, sales, and customer success
- Limited experience with account-based strategies

## Approach

### The ABM Trinity Framework

Implementing my ABM Trinity framework that aligns three critical elements:

1. **Account Selection** - Data-driven ICP refinement and account scoring
2. **Engagement Strategy** - Personalized campaigns across channels
3. **Sales & Marketing Alignment** - Shared metrics and coordinated outreach

### Current Focus Areas

- Building the account scoring model
- Designing tiered engagement playbooks
- Implementing technology stack integrations
- Training sales team on ABM collaboration

## Progress to Date

- Account selection criteria defined and validated
- Tier 1 engagement playbook in development
- Initial pilot with 25 accounts underway
- Early signals showing 4x engagement vs. traditional outreach

## What's Next

- Scale to full 150-account program
- Launch multi-channel campaigns
- Implement intent data integration
- Build attribution and reporting framework
