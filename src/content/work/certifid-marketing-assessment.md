## Overview

Partnered with CertifID to conduct a comprehensive assessment of their marketing operations, identifying bottlenecks and opportunities for optimization across their go-to-market motion.

## Challenge

CertifID had scaled rapidly but their marketing operations hadn't kept pace. They faced:

- Fragmented processes across teams
- Inconsistent lead handoff between marketing and sales
- Limited visibility into campaign performance
- Manual workflows consuming team bandwidth

## Approach

### Phase 1: Discovery & Audit
Conducted stakeholder interviews, process mapping, and technology stack analysis to understand the current state.

### Phase 2: Gap Analysis
Identified key gaps between current operations and industry best practices, prioritized by impact and effort.

### Phase 3: Strategic Roadmap
Developed a phased implementation plan with quick wins and longer-term initiatives.

## Results

Delivered a comprehensive assessment that enabled CertifID to:

- Streamline their lead management process
- Implement automated workflows for campaign execution
- Establish clear KPIs and reporting dashboards
- Create scalable processes for continued growth

## Key Deliverables

- Current State Assessment Report
- Process Documentation Library
- Technology Stack Recommendations
- 12-Month Implementation Roadmap
