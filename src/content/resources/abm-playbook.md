## Overview

This 40-page playbook walks you through every step of building an ABM program from scratch, based on my ABM Trinity framework.

## What You'll Learn

### Chapter 1: Foundation
- Defining ABM for your organization
- Building the business case
- Securing executive sponsorship
- Setting realistic expectations

### Chapter 2: Account Selection
- Developing your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Account scoring methodologies
- Tiering strategies (1:1, 1:few, 1:many)
- Using intent data effectively

### Chapter 3: Engagement Strategy
- Mapping the buying committee
- Content strategy for ABM
- Channel selection and orchestration
- Personalization at scale

### Chapter 4: Sales & Marketing Alignment
- Shared metrics and SLAs
- Account ownership models
- Joint planning cadences
- Communication workflows

### Chapter 5: Technology Stack
- Essential ABM tools
- Integration requirements
- Build vs. buy decisions
- Stack optimization

### Chapter 6: Measurement & Optimization
- ABM metrics that matter
- Attribution in ABM
- Reporting frameworks
- Continuous improvement

## Who This Is For

- Marketing leaders building their first ABM program
- Ops professionals implementing ABM infrastructure
- Sales leaders partnering on account-based strategies
- RevOps teams aligning GTM motions
