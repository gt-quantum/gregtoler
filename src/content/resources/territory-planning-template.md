## What's Included

This territory planning template provides a complete framework for designing, balancing, and managing sales territories. It's been refined across dozens of implementations and includes everything you need to build a data-driven territory model.

### Account Universe Tab

Import your full account list and apply scoring criteria. The template includes:

- Firmographic scoring (employee count, revenue, industry fit)
- Engagement scoring (website visits, content downloads, email opens)
- Fit scoring (technology stack, buying signals)
- Automatic tier assignment (A/B/C accounts)

> "The best territory plans start with rigorous account scoring. This template gives you the framework—you bring the data."

### Capacity Model Tab

Calculate how many accounts each rep can effectively cover based on:

- Expected deal velocity by segment
- Average activities per account per quarter
- Time allocation across account tiers
- Buffer for inbound and expansion opportunities

### Territory Builder Tab

Assign accounts to territories using the drag-and-drop interface. Real-time metrics show you:

- Total addressable pipeline per territory
- Account count by tier
- Geographic concentration
- Workload balance score

### Coverage Analysis Tab

Visual dashboards that reveal optimization opportunities:

- Territory comparison charts
- Whitespace identification
- Overlap detection
- Balance recommendations

## How to Use

**Step 1: Prepare Your Data**

Export your account list from your CRM. The template expects columns for company name, employee count, annual revenue, industry, and any engagement metrics you track.

**Step 2: Configure Scoring**

Open the Settings tab and adjust the scoring weights to match your ICP. The default weights are a starting point—customize based on what predicts success for your business.

**Step 3: Import Accounts**

Paste your account data into the Account Universe tab. The formulas will automatically calculate scores and assign tiers.

**Step 4: Model Capacity**

Use the Capacity Model to determine how many accounts each rep can cover. Be realistic—overloaded territories underperform.

**Step 5: Build Territories**

In the Territory Builder, assign accounts to reps. Use the real-time metrics to ensure balance across territories.

**Step 6: Analyze & Iterate**

Review the Coverage Analysis to identify optimization opportunities. Territory planning is iterative—expect to make adjustments.

## Best Practices

- **Review quarterly, not annually.** Markets change, reps ramp, accounts churn. Static territories become stale.
- **Include both customers and prospects.** Don't forget expansion opportunities in your territory design.
- **Consider geography for field teams.** Travel time is real—cluster accounts when possible.
- **Build in buffer.** Plan for 80% capacity to leave room for surprises.
