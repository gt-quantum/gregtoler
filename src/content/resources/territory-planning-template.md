## What's Included

This territory planning template provides a complete framework for designing, balancing, and managing sales territories.

### Worksheets

1. **Account Universe** — Import and score your full account list
2. **Territory Assignment** — Drag-and-drop territory builder
3. **Capacity Model** — Calculate rep capacity and territory load
4. **Quota Allocation** — Distribute quota based on territory potential
5. **Coverage Analysis** — Identify gaps and overlaps

### Key Features

- **Account Scoring Model** — Pre-built scoring based on firmographic and engagement data
- **Territory Balance Dashboard** — Visual comparison of territory potential vs. capacity
- **What-If Scenarios** — Model different territory configurations
- **Quota Calculator** — Bottom-up quota allocation based on territory attributes

## How to Use

1. **Start with Account Universe** — Import your account list with key attributes (ARR potential, industry, employee count, etc.)
2. **Configure Scoring** — Adjust the scoring weights to match your ICP
3. **Build Territories** — Use the assignment worksheet to group accounts
4. **Validate Balance** — Check the coverage analysis for balance
5. **Allocate Quota** — Use the quota worksheet to distribute targets

## Best Practices

- Review territories quarterly, not just annually
- Include both current customers and prospects in analysis
- Consider travel time and geography for field teams
- Build in 10-15% buffer for territory changes
