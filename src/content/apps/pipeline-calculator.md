## What It Does

The Pipeline Calculator helps you answer the critical question: "Do we have enough pipeline to hit our number?"

### Coverage Analysis

Input your target revenue and current pipeline to see:
- Coverage ratio by stage
- Gap to required pipeline
- Required generation rate

### Capacity Planning

Model your team's capacity to generate and close:
- Deals per rep by stage
- Activity requirements
- Hiring implications

### Scenario Planning

Run what-if scenarios to understand:
- Impact of conversion rate changes
- Effect of deal size variations
- Sensitivity to cycle time

## How to Use

1. **Input Your Data** - Enter your revenue target, current pipeline, and historical conversion rates
2. **Review Coverage** - See your coverage ratio and identify gaps
3. **Model Scenarios** - Adjust variables to see how changes impact your target
4. **Set Targets** - Export stage-level targets for your team
