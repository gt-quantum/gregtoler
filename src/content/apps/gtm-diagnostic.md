## What You'll Learn

The GTM Diagnostic evaluates your go-to-market motion across the dimensions that matter most for B2B growth:

### Strategy & Positioning
- Market segmentation clarity
- Competitive differentiation
- Value proposition alignment

### Demand Generation
- Channel mix effectiveness
- Lead quality and volume
- Conversion efficiency

### Sales Motion
- Sales process maturity
- Win rate optimization
- Deal velocity

### Customer Success
- Onboarding effectiveness
- Retention and expansion
- Advocacy and referrals

## How It Works

1. **Complete the Assessment** - Answer 48 questions across 12 dimensions (takes ~15 minutes)
2. **Review Your Score** - See how you compare to benchmarks and identify gaps
3. **Get Your Roadmap** - Receive prioritized recommendations based on your results
4. **Track Progress** - Re-assess quarterly to measure improvement
