## What's Included

The RevOps Toolkit is a comprehensive collection of everything you need to run a professional revenue operations function.

### Templates

- **Pipeline Review Template** - Weekly pipeline review format with automated insights
- **Forecast Model** - Weighted forecasting with scenario planning
- **Territory Planning Workbook** - Account scoring and assignment framework
- **Lead Scoring Matrix** - Configurable lead scoring model
- **Handoff Checklist** - Marketing-to-sales handoff documentation

### Frameworks

- **Data Model Documentation** - Standard format for documenting your CRM schema
- **Process Mapping Template** - Visualize and optimize your GTM processes
- **Metrics Dictionary** - Standardized definitions for common RevOps metrics

### Dashboards

- **Executive Dashboard** - Board-ready metrics and visualizations
- **Funnel Analytics** - Stage-by-stage conversion analysis
- **Rep Performance** - Activity and outcome tracking by rep

## Who It's For

The toolkit is designed for:
- RevOps leaders building or scaling their function
- Sales ops professionals looking for best practices
- Founders/operators who need to professionalize their GTM
