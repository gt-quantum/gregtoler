## The Problem with Lead Scoring

Most lead scoring models are built once and never updated. They're based on assumptions rather than data, and they don't account for changing buyer behavior.

Lead Scoring Builder solves this with:

### Data-Driven Modeling

Build scores based on actual conversion data:
- Import historical leads and outcomes
- See which attributes correlate with conversion
- Weight factors based on predictive power

### Continuous Optimization

Keep your model accurate over time:
- A/B test new scoring models
- Automatic decay for stale leads
- Regular correlation analysis

### Seamless Deployment

Push models to your CRM with confidence:
- Preview score changes before deployment
- Gradual rollout with monitoring
- Rollback capability if needed

## How It Works

1. **Connect Your Data** - Import leads and conversion data from your CRM
2. **Analyze Patterns** - See which attributes predict conversion
3. **Build Your Model** - Drag and drop to create scoring rules
4. **Test & Validate** - A/B test against current model
5. **Deploy** - Push to CRM with one click
