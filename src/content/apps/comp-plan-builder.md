## Overview

Comp plans are the most powerful lever in sales. This template helps you design plans that motivate the right behaviors without blowing your budget.

### Template Sections

**Base Structure**
Define OTE, base/variable split, and quota. The template supports multiple role types (AE, SDR, AM, etc.) with different structures.

**Accelerators**
Model tiered accelerator rates and thresholds. Visualize payout curves and compare against industry benchmarks.

**SPIFs & Bonuses**
Add strategic incentives for product launches, new logos, multi-year deals, or other priority behaviors.

**Scenario Modeling**
See how different performance distributions affect total comp expense. Model best/worst/expected cases.

**Plan Comparison**
Evaluate multiple plan designs side-by-side. Understand trade-offs between motivation and cost control.
