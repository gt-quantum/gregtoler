## Overview

Most GTM teams are running 15-30+ tools. This audit template helps you understand what you have, what you're actually using, and where you can optimize.

### Audit Sections

**Inventory**
Catalog every tool in your stack with owner, cost, contract dates, and primary use case. The template includes a pre-built category taxonomy.

**Adoption Analysis**
Score each tool on actual usage vs. licensed seats. Identify shelfware and underutilized capabilities.

**Integration Health**
Map data flows between systems. Flag broken integrations, manual workarounds, and single points of failure.

**Cost Analysis**
Calculate true cost of ownership including implementation, maintenance, and opportunity cost. Compare against benchmarks.

**Optimization Roadmap**
Prioritize consolidation opportunities and capability gaps. Build a phased plan for stack optimization.
