## Overview

Defining your ICP isn't a one-time exercise—it's an ongoing process of refinement. This worksheet provides the structure to do it right.

### Sections

**Firmographic Analysis**
Define the company-level attributes that correlate with success: industry, size, growth rate, geography, and organizational structure.

**Technographic Fit**
Map the technology stack indicators that signal readiness and fit. Identify must-have integrations and competitive displacement opportunities.

**Behavioral Signals**
Document the engagement patterns and intent signals that indicate active buying cycles. Prioritize signals by predictive strength.

**Negative Indicators**
Just as important as knowing who to target is knowing who to avoid. Build your disqualification criteria.

**Validation Framework**
Test your ICP against historical data. The worksheet includes formulas to score your existing customers and validate your assumptions.
