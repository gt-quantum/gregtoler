## Overview

The QBR template provides a proven structure for presenting quarterly performance to executives. Each slide is designed to answer the questions leadership actually cares about.

### What's Included

**Performance Section**
- Quota attainment and trends
- Revenue by segment, region, and product
- Year-over-year comparisons

**Pipeline Section**
- Current pipeline by stage
- Coverage ratios and gap analysis
- Forecast confidence breakdown

**Analysis Section**
- Win/loss patterns and root causes
- Competitive insights
- Rep performance distribution

**Forward-Looking Section**
- Next quarter priorities
- Resource and budget asks
- Risk factors and mitigation
