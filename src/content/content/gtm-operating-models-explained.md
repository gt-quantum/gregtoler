## The Decision Framework

Choosing a GTM model isn't about what's trendy — it's about matching your model to:

1. **Product complexity** — Can users get value without assistance?
2. **Deal size** — Does the ACV justify sales involvement?
3. **Buyer type** — Technical vs. business buyers have different expectations
4. **Market maturity** — Are you creating a category or competing in one?
