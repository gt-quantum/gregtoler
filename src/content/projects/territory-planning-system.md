## The Challenge

The sales team was struggling with territory imbalance. Some reps were overloaded with high-potential accounts while others were starved for opportunity. The annual planning process took months and still resulted in mid-year adjustments.

## Approach

We built a territory planning system that could be updated dynamically as the business evolved.

### Account Scoring Model

Developed a multi-factor scoring model incorporating:

- Firmographic fit (size, industry, tech stack)
- Behavioral signals (engagement, intent data)
- Historical conversion rates by segment

### Coverage Model

Created a coverage model that balanced:

- Total addressable opportunity per territory
- Number of accounts per rep
- Geographic considerations for field roles
- Segment expertise matching

### Planning Tool

Built a custom planning tool that allowed sales leadership to:

- Model different territory scenarios
- See real-time balance metrics
- Make adjustments and see downstream impact

## Results

The new system delivered immediate improvements:

- **100% account coverage** (no orphaned accounts)
- **0.92 balance score** across territories (up from 0.64)
- **30% faster ramp** for new reps with clearer books

## Ongoing Use

The tool is now used quarterly for territory reviews and annually for full replanning. The sales ops team can make adjustments in hours instead of weeks.
