## The Challenge

The company had a strong sales motion but was missing the self-serve opportunity. Competitors were gaining ground with freemium offerings, and the product was well-suited for bottom-up adoption.

## Approach

We designed a PLG motion that would complement—not replace—the existing sales-led approach.

### Freemium Model Design

Defined the free tier with careful consideration:

- Enough value to demonstrate the product
- Clear upgrade triggers tied to team growth
- Usage limits that encourage natural expansion

### Product-Qualified Lead Framework

Built a PQL scoring system based on:

- Feature adoption patterns
- Team size and growth velocity
- Engagement frequency and depth
- Integration usage

### Sales Handoff Process

Created a seamless handoff from product to sales:

- Automated alerts when accounts hit PQL thresholds
- Context-rich notifications with product usage data
- Warm handoff templates for SDRs

## Results

Within eight months of launch:

- **$2.1M in self-serve ARR** from new freemium motion
- **12% PQL conversion rate** to paid plans
- **45% higher expansion revenue** from PLG-sourced accounts

## Key Learning

The biggest unlock was giving sales visibility into product usage. Once they could see which accounts were actively using the product, outreach became dramatically more effective.
