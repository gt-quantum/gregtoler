## The Challenge

The marketing team was generating high volumes of leads, but conversion to enterprise deals remained low. Sales was skeptical of marketing-sourced pipeline and preferred to work their own networks.

## Approach

We designed an ABM program that would align marketing efforts with sales priorities and demonstrate measurable impact on enterprise pipeline.

### Target Account Selection

Worked with sales to define the ideal customer profile and select target accounts:

- Identified 200 tier-1 accounts with highest potential
- Defined engagement thresholds for progression
- Created shared visibility into account activity

### Campaign Architecture

Built a multi-channel campaign structure:

- Personalized content by account tier
- Coordinated outreach between SDRs and marketing
- Executive event programming
- Custom landing pages for key accounts

### Measurement Framework

Established clear metrics and attribution:

- Account engagement scores
- Influenced pipeline by account tier
- Sales team adoption and satisfaction

## Current Status

The program launched in Q4 2024. We're currently in the optimization phase, adjusting targeting and messaging based on early engagement data.

## Early Signals

Initial results are promising:

- 3x engagement rate on personalized content
- 40% of tier-1 accounts showing active engagement
- Positive feedback from sales on lead quality
